This Privacy Policy describes how Findly ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses,
and stores information when you install and use the App on your Shopify store.

## 1. Information we collect

When you install the App, we collect and store:

- **Store information**: your store's myshopify.com domain and the API access
  credentials Shopify issues to the App. These are required for the App to
  function and are never shared with third parties.
- **Catalog data**: a cached copy of your store's product tags and collection
  names (handles and titles). This powers the tag and collection suggestions
  in the quiz builder. It contains no customer data and no product pricing or
  inventory details.
- **Quiz configuration**: the quizzes, questions, answers, design settings and
  translations you create in the App.

When shoppers interact with a quiz on your storefront, we collect **anonymous,
aggregated statistics only**:

- Quiz views, quiz completions, and which answers were selected. These records
  are **never linked to a shopper's identity** — we do not collect names, email
  addresses, IP addresses, or any other personal information about your
  customers.
- If you enable in-quiz results with conversion tracking, we record the order
  ID, order total and currency of orders attributed to a quiz (via Shopify's
  orders webhook). We do not store any customer details from those orders.

The storefront quiz widget uses the browser's localStorage for a single,
non-identifying value: the time a popup quiz was last shown, used only to
respect your popup frequency settings. No cookies are set and no cross-site
tracking of any kind takes place.

## 2. How we use information

We use the information above solely to provide the App's features: displaying
quizzes on your storefront, matching answers to your products, and showing you
analytics about your own quizzes. We do not sell, rent, or share any data with
third parties, and we do not use it for advertising.

## 3. Data retention and deletion

- Quiz statistics are kept for a rolling **12 months**; older records are
  deleted automatically.
- You can delete the statistics for any quiz at any time using the "Reset
  stats" / "Reset analytics" controls inside the App.
- When you uninstall the App, Shopify notifies us, and **48 hours after
  uninstall we permanently delete all data associated with your store** —
  quizzes, settings, statistics, catalog cache and credentials — in line with
  Shopify's mandatory data deletion webhooks.

## 4. GDPR and privacy requests

The App implements all of Shopify's mandatory privacy webhooks:

- **Customer data request**: because quiz statistics are anonymous and never
  linked to a customer, we hold no customer-identifiable data to export.
- **Customer data erasure**: likewise, there is no customer-identifiable data
  to erase.
- **Shop data erasure**: all store data is permanently deleted as described in
  section 3.
