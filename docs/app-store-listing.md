## 1. Nume aplicație

**Findly ‑ Product Finder Quiz**

(păstrează cuvintele "quiz" și "product finder" în nume — sunt termenii pe care
comercianții îi caută în App Store)

## 2. Introducere (tagline, ~100 caractere)

> Turn undecided visitors into buyers with a quick quiz that recommends the right products.

Variantă de rezervă:

> Ask 3 simple questions, show shoppers exactly what to buy — with Add to cart built in.

## 3. Descriere lungă (App details)

> Most visitors leave because they don't know what to choose. Findly fixes that.
>
> Build a short quiz in minutes — no code needed. Ask shoppers a few simple questions
> ("Who is it for?", "What's the occasion?", "What's your budget?") and Findly matches
> their answers to your products using the tags you already have, plus real price
> filtering.
>
> Show the matched products right inside the quiz with Add to cart and an optional
> quantity selector, or send shoppers to your theme's own search results. Place the
> quiz wherever it works best: on your homepage, on a specific collection page, as a
> popup with a floating "Take the quiz" button, or drop it anywhere with a theme block.
>
> Every quiz is tracked: views, completions, most popular answers, add-to-carts and
> revenue — so you always know what your quiz is earning.
>
> Works with any theme, in any language: every piece of storefront text is editable.

## 4. Features (bullets, ~80 caractere fiecare)

1. Build a quiz in minutes — no code, with a live preview while you edit
2. Match products by tags and price range — strict or flexible matching
3. In-quiz results with Add to cart, quantity selector and conversion tracking
4. Homepage, collection page, popup or custom placement — your choice
5. Full design control and 100% translatable storefront text

## 6. Logo — prompturi AI

Direcție: simbol simplu, flat, pe fundal plin, lizibil la 60×60px. Fără text în
interior (sau doar litera F). Concept: lupă + bifă / balon de conversație cu bifă
(= "găsit ce căutai").

Prompt 1 (Ideogram/Midjourney):
> Minimal flat app icon, magnifying glass merged with a checkmark, rounded square
> background, two-color palette (deep charcoal + warm coral), no text, no gradients,
> centered, simple geometric shapes, Shopify app icon style

Prompt 2:
> Flat minimal logo, speech bubble containing a checkmark, soft rounded corners,
> single accent color on cream background, app icon, ultra simple, no text

Prompt 3 (doar litera F):
> Letter F monogram app icon, geometric, bold rounded sans-serif, solid background,
> flat design, two colors only, no shadows, no text besides the letter

Verifică după generare: arată bine micșorat la 60px? Nu seamănă cu logo-ul altei
aplicații de quiz (Octane AI, Quiz Kit, Lantern)?

## 7. Checklist înainte de capturi

- [ ] Magazinul de test are 10+ produse cu poze bune (importă catalogul floriei)
- [ ] Quiz-ul demo are 3 întrebări cu texte realiste, fără typo-uri
- [ ] Culorile widget-ului setate să arate premium (nu default-urile alb/negru)
- [ ] Grid width pe "Extra wide", 3-4 produse pe rând, imagini Portrait
- [ ] Câteva completări + add-to-carts de test ca Analytics să aibă cifre
- [ ] Browser fără bookmarks bar / taburi personale la captură (Ctrl+Shift+B)
- [ ] Rezoluție mare la captură (fereastră maximizată, zoom 100%)
